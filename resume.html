Dr. ChungGang Li is an Associate Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at National Cheng Kung University in Taiwan. He also holds additional positions as an Invited Associate Professor at Kobe University's Department of Computational Science and a Visiting Researcher at RIKEN Center for Computational Science in Japan. ChungGang Li earned his Ph.D. in Engineering from National Chiao Tung University in Taiwan in 2011, under the guidance of Professor Wu-Shung Fu.

After completing his Ph.D., ChungGang Li worked as a postdoctoral researcher in Professor Tsubokura's research group at RIKEN Advanced Institute for Computational Science in 2012. In 2016, he joined Kobe University as a Project Assistant Professor, and he was also appointed a Visiting Researcher at RIKEN Center for Computational Science. In 2018, he was promoted to Associate Professor at Kobe University. He has also been a Visiting Scholar at the University of Southern California.

Dr. ChungGang Li's research interests include:
● Natural convection with high temperature differences
● Numerical methods for low-speed compressible flows
● Computational aeroacoustics
● Turbulence models
● Large-scale simulations on supercomputers
